Description of the Project:

The project is an innovative web app designed to help users learn the English language by integrating speech recognition technology to improve pronunciation. Leveraging Azure Speech Services, the app provides a powerful speech recognition system that allows users to practice speaking English in real time. As users engage in pronunciation exercises, the app listens to their speech, evaluates it for accuracy, and offers instant feedback on areas that need improvement. This feature ensures that learners not only understand English but also develop the correct pronunciation, which is crucial for effective communication.

In addition to pronunciation practice, the app offers a variety of interactive quizzes to help users reinforce their language skills. These quizzes are designed to test vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation, ensuring that learners get a well-rounded experience. By completing the quizzes, users can track their progress over time and receive personalized recommendations for further study. The quizzes adapt to the learner's skill level, providing an engaging challenge while preventing frustration, allowing learners to feel a sense of accomplishment with each milestone.

The app's front-end is built with React, offering a user-friendly interface that ensures a smooth learning experience. The design focuses on simplicity, allowing users to easily navigate between practice exercises, quizzes, and feedback sections. The app is intuitive and accessible, making language learning enjoyable for beginners and more advanced learners alike. With speech recognition and interactive features, this app aims to revolutionize the way users learn and practice English, giving them the tools they need to improve their language proficiency efficiently.
