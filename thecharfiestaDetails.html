Description of the Project:

This food delivery app, built using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), offers a seamless and efficient way for users to explore, order, and enjoy their favorite food items. The app’s backend is powered by MongoDB Atlas, where food items are securely stored and organized. Users can easily sign up or log in to create an account, allowing them to save preferences, view order history, and manage personal details.

The app provides a comprehensive menu that users can search through by category, such as pizzas, burgers, salads, and desserts. A robust filtering system allows users to refine their search based on dietary needs, including options for vegetarian or non-vegetarian dishes, making it easier to find exactly what they’re craving. As users explore the menu, they can add food items to their cart with a single click, and the app automatically calculates the total price in real-time, taking into account item quantities, selected toppings, and special requests.

The cart feature is designed for a smooth user experience, displaying an itemized list with clear pricing details and providing the option to edit or remove items before checkout. The checkout process is quick and intuitive, allowing users to review their order, select payment options, and confirm delivery details. With its clean, responsive design and real-time data updates, the app ensures a smooth and enjoyable ordering process, giving users the convenience of having their favorite meals delivered to their doorstep with ease.
